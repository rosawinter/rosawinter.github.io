I am a postdoc in number theory at the FernUni Schweiz/UniDistance Suisse.

Previously I was a research associate in the Number Theory Group of King's College London, working with Rachel Newton. Before that I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Nonlinear Algebra group of Bernd Sturmfels at MPI-MiS in Leipzig. I obtained my PhD under the supervision of Martin Bright and Ronald van Luijk at Universiteit Leiden in 2021.

My research interests are mainly in arithmetic geometry, more specifically, in the arithmetic of surfaces. I mostly focus on low-degree del Pezzo surfaces, and study their arithmetic, geometric, and combinatorial aspects. More broadly, I like to explore applications of algebraic geometry and number theory to other areas of mathematics.
