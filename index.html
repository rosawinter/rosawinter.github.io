I am a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Postdoctoral Fellow at Albert-Ludwigs-Universität Freiburg.

Previously I held postdoc positions in the number theory groups of Unidistance Suisse and King's College London, and in the Nonlinear Algebra group at MPI-MiS in Leipzig. I obtained my PhD under the supervision of Martin Bright and Ronald van Luijk at Universiteit Leiden in 2021.

My research interests are mainly in arithmetic geometry, more specifically, in the arithmetic of surfaces. I mostly focus on low-degree del Pezzo surfaces, and study their arithmetic, geometric, and combinatorial aspects. More broadly, I like to explore applications of algebraic geometry and number theory to other areas of mathematics.
